Why Azure?

Compared to any other cloud service provider, Microsoft has a vast global footprint. Larger number of data centers located across the globe ensures greater redundancy. High availability of Azure cloud storage facilities makes it easier for businesses to migrate their data and applications where they want it to be. This global expansion of Microsoft cloud platform makes it possible to offer a service level agreement (SLA) of 99.95% for its customers.With Azure, companies only need to pay for the resources that their applications are using. And, should a company need to increase its number of users or its data storage, for example, Microsoft can simply adjust their rate. With this ‚Äúpay-as-you-go‚Äù approach, businesses only pay for space that they need, instead of having to pay for empty storage that they may never use. Further, Azure promotes cost savings through flexibility
